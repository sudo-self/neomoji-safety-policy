Neomoji - Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Prevention Policy

Neomoji is committed to creating a safe and secure environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation, and we take active steps to prevent, detect, and address any such activities within our platform.

1. Prevention Measures

  • Content Moderation: Our team uses automated systems and manual review to monitor and remove any content that violates our CSAE policy, including images, videos, or text.
  • Age Verification: We strongly discourage and take steps to prevent all users from interacting with inappropriate content.
  • User Education: We provide educational resources within the app about the importance of online safety and the consequences of CSAE.

2. Reporting CSAE Content

We encourage all users to report any suspicious activity or content related to CSAE. If you come across content that may violate our CSAE policy, please report it immediately. Reports can be made via:

  • In-app reporting: Use the ‘Report’ button in the app to flag inappropriate content.
  • Email: You can email us directly at contact@neomoji.com with details of the concern.

Reports will be reviewed promptly, and appropriate action will be taken, including the removal of harmful content and the potential suspension of offending accounts.

3. Partnerships and Compliance

We work closely with law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations to combat child sexual abuse and exploitation. Our app complies with all relevant laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), The Internet Safety Act, and other global child protection laws.

4. User Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of all our users. Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, store, and use data in a manner that respects user rights and complies with applicable regulations.

5. Ongoing Efforts

We continue to review and improve our policies, tools, and partnerships to ensure we provide a safe space for users. Our CSAE prevention measures are regularly updated to address emerging threats and challenges.

For more information about how we protect against CSAE or to report any concerns, please visit our support page at https://www.neomoji.com/support.
